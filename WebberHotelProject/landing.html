Aurora Hotel
Where the city slows down by the sea.

A quiet, design-forward hotel overlooking the water. Sun-washed rooms, soft linens, and just enough distance from everything urgent.

Boutique stays • Late checkout • Thoughtful hospitality

Designed for stillness.

Every room in Aurora faces the horizon. No printed directories, no televisions by default, no noise you didn’t bring with you. Just light, air, and the sound of the tide under your window.
